New York City


Let's go to New York City! Why New York City? You will get to know in a short time, till then follow me.

  1. The Statue of Liberty, the iconic symbol of freedom and democracy that stands on Liberty Island.
  2. Central Park, the largest and most famous urban park in the world, where you can enjoy nature, culture, and recreation.
  3. The Empire State Building, the skyscraper that offers a breathtaking view of the city from its observation deck.
  4. Times Square, the dazzling and bustling hub of entertainment, shopping, and nightlife.
  5. The Metropolitan Museum of Art, the largest and most visited art museum in the United States, home to over two million works of art.
  • Pizza: The ultimate street food.
  • Bagels: A New York staple.
  • Cheesecake: A rich and creamy dessert.
  • Hot dogs: A classic snack.
  • Pretzels: A salty and crunchy treat.
