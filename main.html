Maldives: A Tropical Paradise

The Maldives is a stunning tropical destination known for its crystal-clear blue waters, white sandy beaches, and vibrant coral reefs. It consists of 26 atolls, each offering a unique and mesmerizing experience.


Top 5 Attractions

  1. Kuramathi Island: A paradise for water sports enthusiasts.
  2. Maafushi Village: Experience the local Maldivian culture.
  3. Snorkeling in Banana Reef: Explore the rich marine life.
  4. Dhigurah Island: Known for its long sandy beach.
  5. Sun Island Resort and Spa: A luxurious getaway with overwater bungalows.

Must-Try Foods

  • Mas Huni: A traditional Maldivian breakfast dish with tuna, coconut, and flatbread.
  • Fihunu Mas: Grilled fish marinated in Maldivian spices.
  • Bis Keemiya: A Maldivian version of samosas, filled with fish, coconut, and spices.
  • Dhivehi Biscuit: Sweet biscuits often enjoyed with a cup of tea.
  • Garudhiya: A fish soup served with rice, lime, chili, and onions.
